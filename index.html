A Little About Me

I am a college student currently studying for an undergrad degree in Information Systems. I found early on that I had an affinity for programming. Ever since I was little, I would dabble in editing conditions and creating scenarios in videogame map-makers, and subtly editing a software's UI design to look just the way I want it too. Over time, these things have steered me towards a developers' state of mind. I appreciate a well designed website, and have decided to persue the skills necessary to create beautiful and functional websites.
